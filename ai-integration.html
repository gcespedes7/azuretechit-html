Common Questions

AI Integration FAQ

What does AI integration actually mean for a small business?
It means connecting AI tools to the systems you already use — your CRM, email, calendar, website — so they work together automatically. You stop doing repetitive tasks manually, and the AI handles routing, follow-up, qualification, or content creation in the background.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT?
ChatGPT is a tool you interact with manually. AI integration means building systems that run on their own — triggering automatically when a lead fills out a form, an email arrives, or a deal moves in your CRM. No copy-pasting, no manual prompts.
What tools do you integrate with?
Most major business tools — HubSpot, GoHighLevel, Salesforce, Gmail, Outlook, Slack, Notion, Airtable, Google Sheets, WhatsApp, Calendly, and more. If it has an API or works with Zapier or Make, we can connect it to an AI layer.
How long does an AI integration project typically take?
Most projects go live in 2–4 weeks. A focused chatbot or lead qualification system can be deployed in under 2 weeks. Larger multi-system integrations with custom AI logic typically run 4–6 weeks depending on complexity and the number of tools involved.
Do we need technical staff to maintain the systems you build?
No. We build everything to run without a dedicated technical team. We handle ongoing monitoring and can adjust systems as your business grows. Most clients manage day-to-day use through simple dashboards with no coding required.
